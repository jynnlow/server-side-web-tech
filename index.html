WHO AM I?

A Woman

A Motorcycle Traveller

A Storyteller

A perpetual learner of the world driven by an insatiable curiosity. Undaunted of what lies beyond the comfort zone for raw experiences, I am always setting new travel goals. Travelling has allowed me to witness the beauties and tragedies of this world which were transformative to my outlooks. What I eventually discovered is the gap between our perspectives and reality on the ground in places beyond the borders. Through storytelling, I hope to forge connection, especially in a time when humanity is overshadowed by dooming media portrayal of the outside world.
